Hi! This is my portfolio from Technigo—a 6 month long frontend bootcamp I took this spring. Now I am looking for a position where I can further grow within this new role.

I started out as a freelance graphic designer back in 2007, and with time my interests have steadily shifted to be more technology focused. During my Masters at Royal College of Art in 2017, I started to experiment with code as a design tool, and I have now decided to fully transition into frontend development.

I love the combination of strict logic with the dynamic playfulness that coding offers, as well as the strong and sharing community. As a developer I get to utilise both my creativity and persistence to solve the problem at hand.
