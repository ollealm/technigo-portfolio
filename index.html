A Stockholm based front-end developer and designer. I started as a freelance graphic designer in 2007, and since then my interests and skills have steadily shifted to be more technology based.

During my MA at Royal College of Art in 2017 I started to experiment with coding as a design tool and my interest in programming has now reached the point where I’m fully transitioning into front-end development.

I love the combination of strict logic with dynamic playfulness that coding offers, as well as the strong and sharing community. As a developer I get to combine my creativity and persistence to solve problems at hand.
